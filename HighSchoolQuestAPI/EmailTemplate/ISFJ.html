ISFJ - Defender

ISTJs are Introverted, Sensing, Thinking, and Judging individuals. They are known as "The Inspector" and are characterized by their practicality, attention to detail, and strong organizational skills.

ISTJ Traits:

  • Introverted (I): ISTJs are reserved and prefer spending time alone or with a small group of close friends.
  • Sensing (S): They focus on the present realities and are detail-oriented.
  • Thinking (T): ISTJs make decisions based on logic and objective analysis rather than personal feelings.
  • Judging (J): They prefer order and structure, making plans and sticking to them.

ISTJ Characteristics:

ISTJs are known for their:

  • Reliability and dependability
  • Strong sense of duty and responsibility
  • Attention to detail
  • Logical and analytical thinking
  • Organizational skills

ISTJ Careers:

ISTJs are well-suited for careers that require precision, attention to detail, and organizational skills. Some suitable professions include:

  • Accountant
  • Engineer
  • Project Manager
  • Lawyer
  • Military Officer
